About Us

Real food, real results,
real life

Welcome to Shree Health Coaching Services — founded by Bhavin Patel, a certified health and nutrition coach based in Gujarat. After personally transforming his own health through the power of wholesome food and mindful living, Bhavin made it his mission to help others do the same.

At Shree, we believe in sustainable change — not crash diets or extreme routines. Our approach blends the wisdom of Ayurvedic eating traditions with modern nutritional science to create plans that fit your life, your culture, and your goals.
